Jim Moore

Chief Technology Officer & Managing Director

Jim Moore is the Chief Marketing Officer (CMO) at Turret Labs and has DockEnroll Services in his Portfolio.

His entrepreneurial and technical background enables him to articulate the value of technology to businesses seeking to implement Aviation IT solutions.

Jim has worked in transportation, financial services, retail and manufacturing as an analyst, designer and developer of enterprise applications for over thirty years.

Prior to joining Turret Labs Jim was with Oracle for ten years, helping customers l​​everage the power of Oracle's technology for their businesses, and, he owned a retail hobby store.
